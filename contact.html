FAQ

What should I wear to my photo session?

Wear something you feel comfortable and confident in! I'm happy to provide style guidance and retailer connections if needed.

How long does it take to receive my photos?

You'll receive your edited photos within 2-3 weeks, depending on the size of the project.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes, a deposit of $100 is required to secure your booking. This will also come with a contract and establishing meeting.

Can I bring a friend or family member to the shoot?

Absolutely! Having someone you trust there can help you feel more relaxed. The studio is big enough for all of us.

How do I book a session?

You can book a session by contacting me through the form on my website. Further discussion will take place after the fact.

What types of photography do you offer?

I specialize in portraits, lifestyle, family and event photography, but I'm open to many creative projects, just ask!
